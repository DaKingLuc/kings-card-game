KINGS - Rules

Players: 2

Decks: 2–4 standard decks (no face cards except Aces)

Goal: Reduce your opponent’s HP to 0.

🧰 Setup

  • Each player draws 5 cards from the main deck.
  • HP is agreed on before the game (100–300).
  • Use separate or shared multiplier decks depending on how many decks are available.
  • Coin flip decides who draws first if decks are shared.

🔁 Turn Flow

  1. Both players choose a card and reveal it.
  2. Each draws a multiplier card.
  3. Calculate total using color, suit, and number.
  4. Deal damage equal to the difference in totals.
  5. Discard played cards and draw back to 5.

🧮 Multipliers

  • Same color = ×2
  • Same color + suit = ×5
  • Exact card = attack × multiplier × 5

Ties = No damage. Aces = 11. Max hand = 5 cards.
