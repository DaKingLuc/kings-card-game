How to Play

Each player starts with 5 cards and custom HP (100–300). Play one card each turn, then draw a multiplier card. Match color for x2 bonus, match color + suit for x5 bonus. Exact card match? Attack x Multiplier x 5. Deal damage = difference between totals.

  • Same color = x2
  • Same color + same suit = x5
  • Exact card = Attack × Multiplier × 5
  • Max hand: 5 cards
  • Coin flip for who draws first (if shared deck)
